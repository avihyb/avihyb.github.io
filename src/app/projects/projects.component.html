What It Does

Clients discover and book barbers nearby.
Barbers manage their availability and build a profile.
Shop owners run a digital storefront and manage their chairs.

Platform Features

Location Discovery Find barbers and shops within a dynamically configurable radius.
Multi-Role Engine Operate flawlessly as a client, barber, and owner from a single account.
Booking Lifecycle Full state tracking from initial request through complete payment.
Profile Management Editable profiles, live avatars, and role-specific onboarding flows.
Incoming Pipelines
Stripe Payouts Dynamic Pricing Push Notify Reviews System Analytics Dashboard

Enterprise Security

All data access enforced exclusively at the database layer (Supabase RLS).

Strict role-based permissions preventing client-side manipulations.

Account execution runs at high privilege; clients remain heavily stateless.
